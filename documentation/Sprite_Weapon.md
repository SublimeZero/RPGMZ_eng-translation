# Class: Sprite_Weapon

## Superclass: [Sprite](Sprite.md)

A sprite for displaying weapons (from `img/system/WeaponsX.png`).

In RPG Maker MV, the superclass `Sprite_Base` was used, but it has been removed in RPG Maker MZ.
